## For AI Assistants

When helping with dashboard projects:

1. **Identify framework** - React, Vue, Angular, or HTML?
2. **Check requirements** - Features needed?
3. **Consider team skills** - What does team know?
4. **Evaluate design** - Modern vs traditional?
5. **Check license** - Free vs commercial use
